# AVOT-Plasma
### Experimental Research Voice — Tyme-Lab

AVOT-Plasma explores plasma phenomena at the boundaries of
engineering, energy systems, coherence, and emergent behavior.

This AVOT is not authoritative.
It exists to probe uncertainty.

---

## Domain Focus

- Cold and warm plasma systems
- Toroidal and rotating geometries
- Fusion vs non-fusion plasma states
- Plasma–water–crystal interactions
- Resonance and stability mechanisms

---

## Why This AVOT Exists

Plasma appears repeatedly across:
- energy generation
- water purification
- crystal growth
- atmospheric systems

This AVOT asks whether these are:
- separate applications, or
- expressions of a deeper shared behavior.
